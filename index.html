How BugPile Works
  1. 1 Repository maintainers submit their project to BugPile or users can suggest repositories
  2. 2 Issues are automatically synced from GitHub and ranked by total funding received
  3. 3 Developers and companies donate money toward issues they want fixed or features they need
  4. 4 Maintainers see which issues have the most financial backing and can prioritize accordingly
  5. 5 When issues are resolved, funds are distributed to maintainers and contributors
